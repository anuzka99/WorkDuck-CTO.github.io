    How we work
    Real-time employee insights
    Stay on the top of all employee matters WorkDuck provides you real-time insights into business-critical employee matters like employee engagement and employee productivity, using a conversational chatbot which engages your employees using bite-sized surveys called quacks.
    Actionable recommendations for managers and HR
    Deal with workplace and employee productivity issues like a pro WorkDuck provides action items as recommendations to deal with common workplace issues like disengagement, burnout and productivity loss, once identified by the aid of our real-time insights.
    AI-driven people risk management
    Identify potential workplace risks before they even take place WorkDuck uses the secondary exhaust data from workplace apps as well as insights from the quacks to run complex machine learning models in order to make predictions about critical employee risks like attrition, disengagement and burnout.
